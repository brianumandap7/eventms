Vision

Guided by the core values of Fortitude, Excellence, Uprightness, and Compassion, FEU Cavite Higher Education Department, a leading professional institution, produces future-ready global citizens and champions the development of society.

Mission

  1. Sustains the Founder’s pioneering spirit in education and nation-building;
  2. Implements an OBE-aligned General Education Curriculum and professional programs;
  3. Offers future-ready education through technology-enabled and adaptive learning approaches;
  4. Provides transformational learning; and
  5. Develops industry-responsive and enterprising individuals.
